Google Play Store Metadata
==========================

App Name (max 30 chars):
Meteograph

Short Description (max 80 chars):
Elegant home screen widget showing temperature, rain, and daylight at a glance.

Full Description (max 4000 chars):
Meteograph displays a beautiful, information-dense weather forecast right on your home screen.

SEE THE WEATHER AT A GLANCE
The meteogram shows 46 hours of forecast plus the last 6 hours, with:
• Temperature curve with gradient fill
• Precipitation bars showing rain and snow intensity
• Daylight intensity based on sun position and cloud cover
• Current time marker so you always know where "now" is

DESIGNED FOR YOUR HOME SCREEN
• Clean, minimal design that complements any wallpaper
• Adapts to system light/dark mode automatically
• Material You dynamic colors on Android 12+ devices
• Updates in the background so data is always fresh

RESPECTS YOUR PRIVACY
• No account required
• No ads, no tracking
• Location is only used to fetch weather data
• Weather data from Open-Meteo (free, open-source API)

LOCALIZED
• Temperature in Celsius or Fahrenheit based on your locale
• 12-hour or 24-hour time format
• Translated into 30+ languages

BATTERY FRIENDLY
• Efficient background updates
• Minimal network usage

Perfect for anyone who wants a quick weather overview without opening an app. Just glance at your home screen and know if you need an umbrella or a jacket.
